CHIMERA

Unleash Your Creativity

CHIMERA is a powerful paraphonic synthesizer with advanced modulation, wavetable synthesis, and intuitive controls. Designed for musicians, composers, and sound designers.

Key Features

3 Oscillators

Shape your sound with three fully customizable oscillators.

3 Filters

Control the tone with a set of versatile filters including low-pass, high-pass, and band-pass.

Modular Design

Connect, route, and control different modules for maximum flexibility.

Wavetable Synthesis

Interpolate between waveforms and create dynamic soundscapes.

MIDI Keyboard Integration

Trigger sounds and control your synth with a fully integrated MIDI keyboard.

Reverb & More

Add depth and dimension with real-time reverb and modulation controls.

Technical Specifications

  • Oscillators: 3 handmade custom waveforms, Wavetable Synthesis
  • Filters: 3 filters (Low-pass, High-pass, Band-pass)
  • Envelopes: 2 ADSR envelopes
  • Paraphony: 1 voice per oscillator
  • MIDI Input: Full MIDI control (keyboard integration)
  • Control: 1 Pitch controller, 1 Reverb knob, Modular connections
  • Display: High-resolution screen to view waveform
  • Connectivity: MIDI, USB, Audio Out
  • Power Supply: 12V DC (Adapter Included)
  • Dimensions: 25.5” (L) x 9” (W) x 3” (H)
  • Weight: 3.5 kg (7.7 lbs)
